## 📊 Analysis Overview

A retail transactions dataset containing **500 records** was analyzed to understand sales performance across:

- 🌍 Regions
- 📦 Products & Categories
- 🛍️ Sales Channels

### Tools Used

| Tool       | Purpose                              |
|------------|--------------------------------------|
| **SQL**    | Data preparation and aggregation     |
| **Power BI** | Interactive one-page dashboard design |

### Key Activities

- ✅ Calculated key KPIs — Total Revenue, Total Transactions, Total Customers, and Average Order Value
- ✅ Analyzed **monthly sales trends** to identify seasonality and peak periods
- ✅ Evaluated **region-wise** and **sales-channel-wise** contributions to total revenue
- ✅ Identified **top-performing products** and monitored category-wise performance trends

## 💡 Key Business Insights

| # | Insight | Details |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | 📈 **Stable Overall Revenue** | Consistent performance with noticeable peaks during mid-year, indicating seasonal demand |
| 2 | ⚖️ **Balanced Regional Contribution** | All regions contribute relatively evenly to total revenue |
| 3 | 🎯 **Revenue Concentration** | Top products account for a large portion of overall sales |
| 4 | 💻 **Channel Performance** | Online and offline channels complement each other; online performs better in certain months |
| 5 | 🔄 **Category Dynamics** | Category-level trends suggest changing customer preferences |
